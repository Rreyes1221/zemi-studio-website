Before your appointment

Everything you need for the best results.

A few thoughtful steps will help you feel comfortable and allow us to give your brows and lashes the care they deserve.

01

Arrive with clean brows & lashes

Please avoid mascara, brow makeup, or heavy eye makeup.

02

Skip oil-based products

Avoid oils, balms, or heavy skincare around the eye area for 24 hours.

03

Remove contact lenses

Please take them out before your appointment if receiving a lash service.

04

Wear your hair down

Loose hair allows for a more comfortable treatment.

05

Avoid strong fragrances

Many guests are sensitive to strong scents.

06

Running late?

Please text us as soon as possible so we can accommodate you if we’re able.
